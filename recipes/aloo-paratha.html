Aloo-paratha

Description

Aloo Paratha is a popular North Indian stuffed flatbread made with whole wheat flour and a spiced potato filling. Itâ€™s hearty, flavorful, and often enjoyed for breakfast or lunch. Served hot with butter, yogurt, or pickle, itâ€™s a comfort food that combines the wholesomeness of wheat with the warmth of spiced potatoes.

Ingredients

  • For the dough:

    1. Whole wheat flour
    2. Oil,water,salt

  • For the stuffing:

    1. Potatoes
    2. Ground Spices
    3. Coriander leaves
    4. Green chili n Ginger

  • Optional Ingredients

    1. Butter n Yogurt
    2. Pickles

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing whole wheat flour, salt, water, and a little oil.
  2. Boil and mash potatoes, then mix with onion, green chili, ginger, coriander leaves, and spices.
  3. Divide the dough into balls and roll each into a small disc.
  4. Place a portion of the potato stuffing in the center, fold, and seal.
  5. Roll out gently into a flat paratha.
  6. Cook on a hot tawa with oil or ghee until golden brown on both sides.
  7. Serve hot with butter, yogurt, or pickle.
